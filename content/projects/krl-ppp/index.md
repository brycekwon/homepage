## Overview

In collaboration with the *Kimball Research Lab*, our team developed a modular pipeline for
processing and analyzing biomedical signals collected from various sensors, including ECG, SCG,
and PPG. The system is designed to provide a robust framework for analyzing physiological signals
and identify potential patterns or correlations indicative of hypovolemia.
